## 📌 Project Overview

This Digital Literacy Project focuses on understanding the concept of digital literacy and its importance in a student’s academic and professional life covering online tools, professional communication, and cybersecurity awareness.

---

## 📂 Project Structure

### Task 1 – Digital Literacy Infographic
I created a one-page infographic using Canva to explain key concepts of this project such as digital literacy, useful tools, online safety, professional presence, and email etiquette.


### Task 2 – Digital Portfolio
I set up and updated profiles on GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle building a professional digital identity and to showcase my skills.


### Task 3 – Platforms
I solved some coding problems earning a badge and created a **Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz** using Google Forms.

👉 Google Form Link: **https://forms.gle/ymhS41oVGu1t9LJb8**


### Task 4 – Email Etiquette
I drafted 2 professional emails: requesting a submission extension and one for an internship. I also created a checklist of social media do’s and don’ts to understand effective communication and responsible online behavior.


### Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness
I prepared a case study on UPI fraud using a realistic fictional scenario and created a prevention checklist to understand common cyber threats and safety measures.
