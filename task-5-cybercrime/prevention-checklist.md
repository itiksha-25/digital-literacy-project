# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

---

## 🔐 General Safety Tips

1. Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, and passwords with anyone
2. Always check the identity of the person before making any payment.
3. Never click on unknown and suspicious links
4. Always check payment requests carefully before approving them
5. Enable two-factor authentication for more robust security
6. Always use trusted and secure apps for making online payments
7. Never use public Wi-Fi for making online payments
8. Always update your apps and devices regularly  

---

## 💳 UPI Safety Tips

- Never approve “collect requests” unless and until you are sure of the sender
- Always remember that you need not enter your UPI PIN if you are receiving money
