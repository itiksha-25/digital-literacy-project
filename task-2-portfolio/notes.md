# Task 2 – Digital Portfolio

I made and maintained profiles on Kaggle, LinkedIn, and GitHub. Professionals and students use these platforms to showcase their work, interact with others, and establish a powerful online presence.

---

### Platforms Used

I set up the following platforms:

- **GitHub** – Used to store, manage and display coding projects. It helps in tracking progress and building a project portfolio.  
- **LinkedIn** – A professional networking platform where I added my educational details and created a basic profile to connect with others.  
- **Kaggle** – A platform for data science and machine learning, where I can explore datasets and practice skills.  

---

Each platform has its own use, and when you use them all together, they help you build a full digital profile. Making these accounts helped me realize how important it is to have a professional online presence.

In the next years, I will add my new projects to these platforms, get better at what I do, and make new connections. This will help me build a strong portfolio that I can use for internships and other chances in the future.
