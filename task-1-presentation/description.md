# Task 1 – Digital Literacy Infographic

I made a one-page infographic for this task to help students understand the concept of digital literacy and its importance. Because Canva offers easy access to templates and icons that help in the clear and visually appealing presentation of information, I chose it as my design tool.

---

### Topics Covered

The infographic includes the following important subjects:

1. What is digital literacy  
2. Useful digital tools  
3. Safe internet practices  
4. Professional online presence  
5. Email etiquette  

---

In order to make the content easy to read and comprehend at a glance, I focused on keeping it brief by using points and structuring each section.

One challenge I faced while making the infographic was deciding how to include all the important information without making the design look overcrowded. This helped me understand the importance of clarity and simplicity in visual communication.

---

Overall, this task improved my ability to present information effectively and made me more aware of how digital literacy plays an important role in a student’s academic and professional life.
