# Social Media Do's and Don'ts

## ✅ Do's
- Develop a professional profile with an appropriate username and profile picture  
- Always think before you post and make sure that your post reflects positively on you  
- Always respect others and behave in a polite manner in comments and messages  
- Always keep your information private and secure  
- Always make use of social media to learn and share information that can benefit others  

---

## ❌ Don'ts
- Avoid sharing sensitive information such as phone numbers, addresses, passwords, etc.  
- Avoid sharing inappropriate information  
- Avoid engaging in any argument and being disrespectful online  
- Avoid sharing false information  
- Avoid spending too much time on social media at the cost of being unproductive
